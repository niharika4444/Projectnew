Hostel

The University of Kerala provides hostels to residential students (both - boys and girls). There are six hostels functioning under University of Kerala at Kariavattom campus and at Thycaud, Thiruvananthapuram. Two Mens (PG & Research) and Three Womens (Two PG & One Research) Hostels at Kariavattom Campus and one Womens hostel at Vazhuthacaud. The hostel provides admission to UG/PG/M.Phil/Research students.
